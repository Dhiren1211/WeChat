timro aasu timro dukha sabai maile khosi liye 
dosh mero lu aaja pani maile aafai lai kosi diye 
tha thiyo pida hunxa antye ma tyei nih sambanda bare sochi rahe!!!


merai dosh aasu dekhna na sakhnu timro aakhama 
dosh merai timro sapana pura garna aafno sapana launu aakha kakha maa
dosh bhayo mero timro baran garu jhyaure bhaka ma 
dosh merai xal kapat na dekhnu timro aakha ma 


aafi sanga youdha aaja kata maar
galti gara pani maphi didai gaye lagatar
mitho khel khelna sipalu timi samajhdar
premikako biyog ma premi pagal yeta ko samachar
doshi ma  aaja thunina puge man ko karagar
